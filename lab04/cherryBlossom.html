A View of Cherry Blossoms

Published March 11, 2026 by Emmanuel A Onyike

Image Description

This image captures the breathtaking beauty of cherry blossoms in full bloom, taken from section of the Washington D C tidal basin, taken on March 24, 2024 at about 4:59 PM Eastern Time. This picture reminds me of a peaceful moment in nature. The delicate pink petals create a stunning contrast against the clear blue sky, evoking a sense of tranquility and wonder. The vibrant colors and intricate details of the blossoms make this scene truly mesmerizing, reminding us of the fleeting nature of beauty and the importance of cherishing every moment.

Image File Type Information

The image file type is PNG format. It was formerly a JPEG file but for the purposed of this assignment, I converted it to PNG using windows image editor. It has a resolution of 4032X3024 pixels and a file size of approximately 12.2 MB. It was captured using a motorola motorola edge plus 2023.

Why I chose this image

I chose this image because it is almost that time of the year when millions of people travel to Washington DC to witness the annual cherry blossom festival. What a sweet coincidence that we are doing this project at almost the second anniversary of the day the picture was taken. The image captures the essence of spring and the beauty of nature.
